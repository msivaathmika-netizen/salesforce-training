# salesforce-training
Why companies use Salesforce:
saves time
keeps data organized
improves customer service
helps increase sales
Many big companies use Salesforce because it works through the internet (“cloud”), so teams can use it from anywhere..

Salesforce is a software used by companies to manage customers, sales, and support in one place. It’s like an online customer management system

Salesforce is the world’s #1 AI CRM. Our leading customer relationship management platform brings together people, AI agents, apps, and data on one open, trusted platform. 

Whether you’re a small business getting started with CRM or an enterprise transforming how teams work, Salesforce helps every organization become an Agentic Enterprise — where humans and AI agents drive customer success together.
